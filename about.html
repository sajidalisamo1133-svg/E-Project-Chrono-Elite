How We Started

Back in 2015 we began in a small space with big ideas. Thought people still wanted real watches even with phones everywhere. Used all our money to get the first parts from Switzerland. Worked crazy hours to make it happen.

Hard Times

2016 to 2018 was tough. Prices kept going up, shipments got lost, money was always tight. Almost quit multiple times. Had to learn everything the hard way about making watches that actually work.
Started fixing other people's watches just to pay bills. Learned more from breaking and fixing watches than from any manual.

How We Make Watches

Each watch takes about three weeks. We don't rush things. Every piece gets tested properly before we put it together. No shortcuts.
Good materials from different places - glass from Germany, leather from Italy, mechanics from Switzerland. We put it all together here and make sure it's right.

Shipping Stuff

Had lots of problems with shipping at first. Broken watches, lost packages, you name it. Now we pack everything super carefully in special boxes.
We ship everywhere and handle the paperwork. Usually takes about a week. If something goes wrong we fix it fast.
